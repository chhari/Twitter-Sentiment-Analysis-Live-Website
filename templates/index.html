About Twitter sentiment analysis

Sentiment Analysis helps in determining how a certain individual or group responds to a specific thing or a topic. Usually, surveys are conducted to collect data and do statistical analysis. With Twitter, it works by deriving tweets containing references to the preferred topic, computing the sentiment polarity and strong points of each tweet, and then combining the results of all such tweets. A lot of companies make use this to gather public opinion on their service and products, and make data centric decisions.
